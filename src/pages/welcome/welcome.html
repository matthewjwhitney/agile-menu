WELCOME TO TRAIL CREST BREWING!

Since Trail Crest Brewing sources all of its food from local farms and ranches and brews all of its beer in-house, our menu changes frequently! Instead of killing many trees printing hardcopy menus everytime our menu changes, we are one of the first restaurants to utilize a digital menu. When we introduce or run out of a beer or a food item it is added to or removed from the menu in the blink of an eye!

For every item on the menu, we provide information on the supplier so you know that you are eating a locally grown, naturally-raised product!

We would appreciate your feedback!
